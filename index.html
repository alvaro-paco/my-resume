My name is Alvaro, I've 31 years old, I'm from São Carlos, city of Sao Paulo state. I was born and raised here, I studied all my childhood and college periods in the city as well. I come from a middle-class family and parents who have always prioritized their studies, so they made an extra effort so that they could have the best education possible. After a few months, I lost interest in the course because I did not have a perspective of working on something that really was going to make me professionally, besides the city itself does not please me very much, and I gave up. After returning to Sao Carlos I went back to study, because I did not know for sure which career to pursue. It was then that I chose to study Computer Science. I have always had a lot of interest in the area, and I was already doing some freelance with website development and e-commerce. At the end of 2009 I was approved at USP (University of São Paulo), in the course of Bsc. Computer Science at the campus of São Carlos. I think it was the best option for me, because besides studying and working with something that really makes me happy I could be close to my family members during degree. In 2010, I worked on some more projects for third parties and in 2011 I decided to start internships. I started working at a company called BrProg. I worked there for a year there and by the end of 2012 they ended up closing.
